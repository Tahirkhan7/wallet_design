Your Keys.

CryptoVault is a non-custodial desktop wallet giving you complete control over your assets across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and BNB Chain — with military-grade encryption.

🔐

True Self-Custody

Private keys never leave your device. All signing happens locally in isolated process.

⛓️

Multi-Chain Support

Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, BSC — powered by TrustWallet Core with BIP44 derivation.

🛡️

AES-256 Encryption

Keystore encrypted with PBKDF2 + AES-256-GCM. Your password never stored anywhere.

📊

Live Portfolio

Real-time balances and USD values powered by CoinGecko API integration.

📱

QR Code Scanner

Scan recipient addresses or generate QR codes for incoming transfers instantly.

⚡

Smart Fee Engine

EIP-1559 dynamic fees for ETH, UTXO selection for BTC, compute units for SOL.
